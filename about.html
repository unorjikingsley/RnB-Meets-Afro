The concerts brings together a community of people from different culture, race and ethnicity who comprise the entertainment industry in a different country every two years.
Next year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advances the cause of afro-pop, free culture and knowledge.
So if you are active and engaged in the worlds of open content and music - Producers, Music Directors, Music Promoters, D.J, gallaries, museums, archieves, governments and foundations, activists, and Content Creatives - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant free society together.
